Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

job-tracker-cli is a personal, open-source command-line tool. This privacy policy describes how the application handles your data.

Google Account Access

This application requests access to your Google Sheets in order to append job application rows to a spreadsheet you specify. The application uses the Google Sheets API (read/write) scope.

  • Access is limited to the specific spreadsheet you configure.
  • No data from your Google account is transmitted to any third-party server.
  • Your OAuth token is stored locally on your machine and is never shared at:
    • macOs: ~/Library/Application Support/job-tracker
    • Linux: ~/.config/job-tracker
    • Windows: AppData/Roaming/job-tracker

Data Collection

This application does not:

  • Collect or transmit any personal data to external servers
  • Use analytics or telemetry of any kind
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Store data outside of your local machine and the Google Sheet you own

Data Stored Locally

The following files are created on your machine:

  • ~/.config/job-tracker/config.json — your spreadsheet ID and preferences
  • ~/.config/job-tracker/token.json — your Google OAuth token (refresh token)

You can delete these files at any time to revoke local access.

Revoking Access

You can revoke this application's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
